Reel One — The Legacy

Frost Media Group spent two decades behind the camera for nonprofits — turning missions into stories, and stories into the support that keeps good work alive.

Hundreds of productions. Boardrooms, galas, field sites, and living rooms. A book — New Media Habits — a workshop built around it, and an operating system for how mission-driven stories get made.

And hiding in plain sight, a lesson that became a thesis: a film crew is the original decentralized workforce. Specialists assembled for the project, trusted with their craft, dissolved at the wrap. It wasn't even my first proof — before the cameras, HomeSmartz thrived as a decentralized mortgage brokerage built on the same conviction. Twenty-five years of building this way taught me how work actually wants to be organized — long before the world had names for it.

That chapter is complete. The craft and the founder moved into everything that came next: proving that work is decentralizing into fractional leadership, global capacity, and agentic AI. If someone sent you here looking for Frost Media Group, you're in the right place. Here's where the work continues.
